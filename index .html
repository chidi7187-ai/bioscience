About the Department

The Department of Biological Science, Faculty of Science, University of Abuja, is dedicated to nurturing future scientists with deep understanding of life and the environment. We provide practical and theoretical knowledge that prepares students to solve biological and ecological problems for a sustainable future.

Our Mission: To empower students through research, innovation, and community development, equipping them with scientific skills and moral integrity to impact society positively.
